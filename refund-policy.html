Refund Policy

Last updated: [Current Date]

1. Digital Product Policy

Since notes are delivered instantly as digital content, we generally do not offer refunds once access is provided.

2. Refund Eligibility

Refunds may be considered in these cases only:

  • Duplicate payment (accidental double charge)
  • Technical failure preventing access (and our support cannot fix it within 24 hours)
  • Wrong product purchased (and no access was used)

3. Non-Refundable Situations

No refunds for:

  • Change of mind after viewing notes
  • Not satisfied with content quality
  • Exam results not as expected
  • Clearing browser data/losing access
  • Wanting notes for different subject

4. Refund Process

To request a refund:

  1. Contact us within 24 hours of purchase
  2. Provide payment transaction ID
  3. Explain the issue clearly
  4. Allow 3-5 business days for processing

5. Contact for Refunds

Use our contact form with subject "Refund Request".
